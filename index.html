In the far future...

...a young black woman finds herself trapped, mentally and physicaly, in the city she was born in. For centuries, the people have been kept inside by a gate with only an inscription to aid them. While our budding protagonist is not one of the lucky few allowed to test their mettle against the gate, she refuses to stay where she is forever. In this video game (still in its early stages), you'll travel to the gate, developing your friendship, skills, and yourself. There are four main paths you can take - but which one is best for you?

This project is the culmination of a study of possibilities of self-realization for black women to more fulfilling, joyful lives. I have taken huge inpispiration from afrofuturistic aesthetics and Audre Lorde's poetry, and also from my own life. I chose the medium of video game character design and fashion because of the representation opportunities and the diversity in physical self-expression.
